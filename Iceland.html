Iceland

Breathtaking Beauty At Every Turn

Last summer, I had the opportunity to travel to Iceland with my mom, sister, and aunt. We rented a camper van and drove around the entire Ring Road, which stretches around the perimeter of Iceland. Each day, we would see the most unbelievable, jaw-dropping thing and think that nothing could possibly top it, but the next day would be even better.

We saw spectacular waterfalls, green canyons, black sand beaches, basalt cliffs, and glaciers. Driving along the road, we could see sheep and horses. We drove down the road and into the town where the skateboarding scene in The Secret Life of Walter Mitty takes place. We also visited a few geothermal spas and, on our last day, we took a ferry to the neighboring Westman Islands. It was a trip I will never forget!
