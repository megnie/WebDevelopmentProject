Oregon

Beaches, Forests, and Lighthouses

A few summers ago, my family went on a trip to Lincoln City, Oregon. It was one of the most beautiful places I have ever been. The beach, surrounded by tall, green forests and a misty sky, with lighthouses in the distance, made for a spectacular view.

We walked along the beaches, went for lots of hikes, and picked the blackberries that grew on the side of the road. One of my favorite hikes was the Knoll, where you hike through a forest and up a mountain until it opens up to a spectacular view of the ocean.
